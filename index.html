About us

What is TACS?
TACS (Texas A&M Computing Society) is the official student chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery and IEEE Computer Society. We encourage professional development by sponsoring industry speakers and professors while providing numerous networking opportunities.
What are the qualifications to join?
At TACS, we welcome anyone! We want to serve as a platform for any student interested in becoming involved in the tech community.
When and where is the next meeting?
Our event information is usually listed on our Google Calendar; However, if by chance certain information is missing, please feel free to contact one of the board members.
What are workshops?
Workshops are a great way for you to get your feet wet with coding. On a given workshop event, we will focus on a precise topic such as the intro to python, and we will demo a simple application that others can follow along to. Our workshops are hosted by either a professor, alum, company rep, or one of our board members!
