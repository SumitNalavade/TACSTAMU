About Us:

TACS is the official student chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery and IEEE Computer Society. We encourage professional development by sponsoring industry speakers and professors while providing numerous networking opportunities.

We host the Spotlight competition for TACS members to show off their awesome creations and side projects. More information can be found on the Spotlight page!
